About us:-

In pursuance with the policies of the Government of India to start Engineering colleges in emerging technologies, Deogiri Institute of Engineering and Management Studies is established at Aurangabad during the academic year 2009-10. Deogiri Institute of Engineering and Management Studies is affiliated to the Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University, Lonere and is developed as per the norms of the All India Council of Technical Education (A.I.C.T.E.), Government of India. The Institute is consistently among the top institutes of Dr. B.A.M. University in examination results. The student-centric teaching learning environment, state of the art infrastructure, well-equipped laboratories, library stacked with good number of books, experienced and dedicated team of faculties are the key features of the Institute.

Department:-

The college offers the following streams/branches:

Stream/Branch Description
Computer Science The Computer Science department offers courses in programming, algorithms, data structures, computer networks, and database systems.
Electrical Engineering The Electrical Engineering department offers courses in electrical circuits, power systems, control systems, and digital signal processing.
Mechanical Engineering The Mechanical Engineering department offers courses in mechanics, thermodynamics, materials science, and manufacturing processes.
Civil Engineering The Civil Engineering department offers courses in structural engineering, geotechnical engineering, transportation engineering, and environmental engineering.

Faculty:-

The faculty at Engineering College consists of highly qualified and experienced professors and lecturers. They are dedicated to providing the best education to their students and are actively involved in research and development in their respective fields.

Research:-

Engineering College has a strong research focus and encourages its faculty and students to engage in research activities. The college has state-of-the-art research facilities and labs to support research activities.

Placement:-

The college has an active placement cell that helps students find suitable employment opportunities after graduation. The placement cell has tie-ups with leading companies in various industries and regularly organizes recruitment drives and job fairs.
